Meet Dr.

Sherif Zaher

Dr. Zaher has a doctorate in clinical psychology, a masters in Kinesiology (sports performance), and a bachelor's in psychology. He’s also a licensed clinical psychologist in the state of CA. and continues to offer his clinical and sport psychology services to clients via telehealth.

Dr. Zaher’s clinical background includes but is not limited to the following positions: junior specialist with UC Davis Children's Hospital, mental health rehabilitation specialist at a crisis residential center, and a psychological assistant at an outpatient mental health clinic. He considers his involvement in a patient's recovery process a great honor and focuses on adopting an eclectic approach that would provide each client with the necessary treatment that would help in overcoming the presenting symptoms experienced. He has a special interest in assisting patients struggling with depression and anxiety-related disorders. Dr. Zaher’s area of expertise is sport psychology, where he has been working with high-level athletes for 20+ years.

Why Sports Psychology?

There are several barriers that are commonly experienced while performing that negatively impact the process, and ultimately the desired outcome. Developing mental skills to navigate and overcome these challenges is how we are able to tap into a state of flow, also commonly known as the zone, where peak performance is achieved. Athletes working on their mindset gain a mental edge on their competition, which is often the key component to winning.
